Privacy Policy

Olympus Chess Academy is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of all individuals who interact with us—whether through our website, mobile platforms, communication channels, or physical sessions. This policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information in accordance with the Information Technology Act, 2000, and other applicable data protection laws in India.

When you register or interact with us through our website, phone, or during offline events, we may collect personal details such as your name, contact number, email address, student age or grade, and payment information. This data helps us manage your registration, provide quality training services, communicate updates, and send reports or receipts. Your information also allows us to better understand your preferences and improve our offerings. We may use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies help us remember user preferences and provide smoother navigation. These do not store sensitive or personally identifiable information. Your personal data is kept strictly confidential and is only shared with authorized staff or trusted service providers when necessary for operational purposes. We may disclose information only if required by law, legal process, or to ensure the safety and integrity of our services. All photographs, videos, or chess games recorded during classes, workshops, or tournaments are considered the intellectual property of Olympus Chess Academy and may be used for educational content, feedback, or promotional purposes. We ensure data security through strict internal protocols and allow you to opt out of non-essential communications at any time. If our mobile app is used, it may request limited access to features such as camera or media for educational interaction. These permissions are used solely to support your learning experience. By choosing to register with Olympus Chess Academy or access our content and services, you consent to the terms outlined in this privacy policy.
